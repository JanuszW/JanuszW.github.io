Squiggles

Squiggles is a masterpiece made with javascript and completed on repl.it. This piece of artwork uses numerous things we learned in class throughout the year. To begin, click the mouse on the canvas; you will witness a line growing across the screen. Continue making lines by clicking. Continue clicking until you reach the end. (Hint - Click more toward the edge of the canvas)
